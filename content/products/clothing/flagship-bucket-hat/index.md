#### A year of refusing to settle

We spent twelve months obsessing over this design and rejecting every version that didn't meet our standards. We wouldn't launch until we had a bucket hat that was light years ahead of the usual mass-produced alternatives. The AS Colour Bucket Hat is the only chassis that made the cut for the _EpicLines_ Flagship Bucket Hat.

#### Zero flop. Max vision

Most bucket hats are so flimsy that the brim collapses in the first gust of wind. We chose this light-to-midweight 100% cotton build because it breathes when the sun is beating down and holds its shape during offshore or onshore winds. The brim is reinforced with heavy stitching detail, protecting your ears and the back of your neck from the sun while cutting the water glare to maximise your vision no matter which break you’ve just pulled up to.

#### Pure Function. The _EpicLines_ fit

Built for the serious nomadic surfer. This is a low-profile fit that sits close and secure, eliminating the flimsy, shapeless feel of inferior bucket hats. With side eyelets for airflow, it’s a solid, reliable essential for those who spend their lives making lines.
