The Professional Choice: Bigger, Better, and Better Value.

The _EpicLines_ Heatseal Tape is the exact same heavy-duty, iron-on material we use in our workshop for professional wetsuit repairs. One of our staff was involved in pioneering the repair of wetsuits at Canterbury Wetsuits and has been using this material for 34 years to repair all of their wetsuits, and of course lots of other items.

While competitors offer smaller kits at a higher premium, we provide a longer professional-grade roll at a lower price, giving you more material to get the job done right.

This will repair many types of rips, tears, and ladders in wetsuits and all sorts of clothing. It is made from 3 layers of high-performance waterproof fabric and is also suitable for use on raincoats, waders, footwear, military uniforms, etc.

## Why It’s the Best Value on the Market:

* **More Coverage:** Our rolls are significantly longer than our competitor’s
* **Workshop Proven:** This isn't just a "home kit"—it’s the industry-standard material we trust for our own repair work
* **Versatile Design:** Perfect for sealing long seams, covering stitching, or reinforcing tears across wetsuits and waterproof gear
* **Winter Conversion:** You can also use it to convert your summer wetsuit into a winter wetsuit simply by applying it to the seams on the inside and saving your self $100's of dollars

## Included in the Kit:

* 1x Heatseal (hot melt) Tape (20mm wide and 3m long)
* Professional Instructions: The same techniques we use in-house for a permanent, watertight bond.

## Compatibility & Safety:

* **Suit Type:** This type of tape is ironed on and will only stick to wetsuits with a nylon covering. i.e., not smoothie suits
* **Seam Care:** Avoid ironing over rubber or liquid (welded) seams, as the heat will melt the existing seam tape.

If you want more than 3 meters, just increase the count in the shopping cart, and we will make the length continuous.
